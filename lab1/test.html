Whispers of the Wind

I stare at code, my mind in dread,
Each tag a puzzle, twisted, spread,
The errors flood, the screen is a blur,
A storm of code I can't deter.

I close a tag, but one remains,
A missing brace, it drives me insane,
The page won't load, the colors fade,
I feel my patience slowly frayed.

Then with a click, the code aligns,
The layout flows, the text refines,
A web emerges, sharp and bright,
A masterpiece born from endless fight.

With joy I see it, smooth and grand,
The perfect site built by my hand,
I take a breath, my heart unfurled,
And whisper softly: Hello, World.
